Sensei Nakamura began his martial arts journey at age 6 in Okinawa, Japan. After earning his 3rd Dan in Japan, he moved to the United States in 1990 to spread authentic karate teachings. He founded Forever Karate Academy in 1995 with the mission of preserving traditional techniques while adapting them for modern self-defense needs.

With over three decades of teaching experience, Sensei Nakamura has developed a unique teaching methodology that emphasizes both physical mastery and mental discipline. His students have gone on to win numerous national and international competitions.

Sensei Martinez is our resident competition expert, having won numerous national titles before transitioning to coaching. His competitive experience brings a unique perspective to our training programs, particularly in sparring (kumite) techniques and tournament strategy.

After earning his black belt at age 16, Sensei Martinez competed internationally for 10 years before joining Forever Karate Academy in 2010. His students consistently place in regional and national competitions, with several earning spots on Team USA.
