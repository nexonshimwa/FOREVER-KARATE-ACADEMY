Our Mission

At Forever Karate, we are dedicated to preserving the ancient art of karate while adapting it to modern self-defense needs. Our mission is to empower individuals through discipline, physical fitness, and mental fortitude.

Our History

Founded in 1995 by Master Hiroshi Tanaka, Forever Karate has grown from a small dojo to an international organization with over 50 locations worldwide. Master Tanaka's vision was to create a karate school that would stand the test of time - hence the name "Forever Karate".

Our Instructors

All our instructors are black belt holders with at least 10 years of teaching experience. They undergo rigorous training and certification to ensure they can provide the highest quality instruction to students of all ages and skill levels.

Training Programs

We offer programs for all ages and skill levels:

  • Little Dragons (ages 4-6)
  • Junior Warriors (ages 7-12)
  • Teen Training (ages 13-17)
  • Adult Programs (ages 18+)
  • Senior Self-Defense (ages 55+)
